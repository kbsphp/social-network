Uzytz Targeting Options

Targeting is one of the most important benefits of Uzytz advertising since it gives you the ability to show your ads to particular types of people who will engage with your content the best and put the most money in your pocket.

Target audiences are also highly customizable, making it easy to target people based on interests/likes, demographics, locations, website, and app engagement, Uzytz groups and so much more.

To ensure the most success with Uzytz ads, you want to make sure you are targeting the right people, acknowledging and connecting with them and giving them a realistic call-to-action.

  • Location: Reach people based on country, state or province, current city or zip code
  • Age and Gender: Narrow your audience based on age and gender
  • Language: Target your ads to specific languages
  • Behaviors: Reach people based on purchase behaviors or intents, device usage and more
  • Connections: Reach people who are connected to your businessâ€™s Uzytz Page, app, event or their friends
  • Custom Audience: Create your ideal audience using pixels, email addresses, phone numbers
  • Lookalike Audience: Reach people who are similar to your audience(s)
